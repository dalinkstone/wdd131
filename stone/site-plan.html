Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to attract new clients and projects and eventually work for those clients to complete the offered projects.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is anyone in any stage of the funnel of freelancing work by stone.enterprises

Dynamic elements

The dynamic elements here will be the scroll from the main page to the contact form and then the firing off of an email from the contact form to the dalin@stone.enterprises email domain.

Content

Home

The home page will display the logo, a brief blurb to draw the eye with a description about what we do and how we can deliver value. The nav bar is going to be vertically displayed on the right side of the screen with only a 'Home' link and a 'Contact Us' link.

Contact Us

The contact us form will have boxes for Name, Return Email, and then a body message. All of the fields will be required and the body message will require at least 100 characters. I would also want to setup captcha too if possible, but I don't want to commit to this because I haven't done any research on how to do it.

Wireframes

Home

The home page should scroll into the Contact Us page ideally. So there is no real pagination, just as you scroll you get to the "Contact Us Page". If this technically does not meet the requirements, then we'll scrape it and just do a linking child page.
